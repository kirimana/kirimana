# Governance

## Stewardship

Kirimana is stewarded by **David Barton Consulting AB** (Sweden), the legal entity that owns the Kirimana trademark and copyrights to original contributions during private preview. Stewardship means: holding the trademark, maintaining the canonical repositories, and convening the maintainer group.

## Long-term path

We are pursuing donation to a neutral foundation — most likely [LF AI & Data](https://lfaidata.foundation/), the same home as the [ODCS / Bitol](https://github.com/bitol-io) standard Kirimana is built on. Foundation discussions are planned for H2 2026 and follow-on through 2027. This document will be updated as that path firms up.

## Licence commitment

Kirimana is licensed under [Apache-2.0](LICENSE) **forever**. We do not relicense. We do not BSL. The licence path is part of why this project exists; weakening it is not on the table.

## Decision-making

During private preview (v0.9 → v1.0):

- Architectural decisions are recorded as ADRs in [`docs/adr/`](docs/adr/) and merged via PR review.
- The maintainer group is small (founder + a handful of design partners). Decisions surface in public PRs whenever they affect public interfaces.
- Commercial-track decisions (paid offerings, partnerships) are kept private and do not appear in the public ADR set.

At GA (v1.0):

- A formal maintainer group of at least three independent maintainers is established.
- Lazy consensus on PRs is the default; contested changes go to maintainer vote.
- The governance model is documented as ADR 002 evolves.
